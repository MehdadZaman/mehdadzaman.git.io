Allergy Alert

Prevent SBU students from having allergic reations at campus dining

There have been numerous allergic reactions on dining locations on campus. AlleryAlert allows students to scan barcodes associated with menu items and immediatley find out whether or not they are allergic to a menu item. Jahin Imtiaz, Justin Chassin, and I hope to collaborate with the Faculty Student Association to mandate QR codes on all campus dining locations and promote safety at Stony Brook!
